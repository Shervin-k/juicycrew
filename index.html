Why Podcast Marketing Works for Brands

Reach a Premium Audience

Podcast listeners are well-educated and high-income, a target group usually difficult to reach through traditional media.

Explain a Complex Product

With the highest attention of any ad format, podcasts are the perfect stage to explain complex products clearly and memorably.

Build Trust and Loyalty

Podcast hosts have authentic bonds with their listeners, so ads feel like a credible recommendation from a close friend.

Bumble

Date on your terms.

For Bumble, we focused on making the app feel like a trusted recommendation from a friend. We partnered with hosts who had especially deep bonds with their audiences, ensuring the message resonated naturally with the right target group. Over the years, as Bumble introduced new features, these campaigns helped explain them in a simple and memorable way — from enhanced safety tools to fresh app benefits — reinforcing Bumble as a safe, evolving space for dating.

ZWILLING

Keep Food 5× longer fresh.

Zwilling’s Fresh & Save system keeps food fresh up to five times longer, but the concept needed to be made tangible. We brought the product to life by placing it in shows where it fit seamlessly into daily routines — from parenting podcasts about kids’ meals, to fitness podcasts about meal prep, to vegan shows about conscious cooking. By aligning real-life stories with the product’s benefits, we reached high-income households and showed the system’s value in everyday life.

AG1

Own your day.

AG1 offers a daily supplement subscription that covers essential nutrition, but the challenge was making a new routine feel natural. We started broad, testing across different podcast verticals, then doubled down on the best-performing shows. Hosts authentically integrated AG1 into their own daily habits, explaining how the ingredients supported wellbeing and energy. By mixing personal stories with education, the campaign reached listeners open to innovative health solutions, with the income and motivation to make AG1 part of their lifestyle.

Armedangels

Fashion with a Purpose.

Armedangels wanted to share their sustainable fashion story in a way that was exciting as well as credible. We selected hosts whose style and values matched the brand, from younger audiences to listeners in their sixties, where authenticity mattered more than demographics. The campaign paired favourite pieces from the new collection with a rare discount code, creating both trust and exclusivity. The result was a combination of genuine storytelling, wider awareness, and measurable conversions for a sustainability-driven fashion brand.
